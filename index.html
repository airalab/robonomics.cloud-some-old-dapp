Robonomics Web Services

RWS overview, part 3: David vs. Goliath

A metaphor that characterizes the situation of the battle between the small and the big and the victory of the small with a single blow.

RWS will provide a truly globally accessible IoT device connectivity network. It's like comparing internet banks with crypto or internet sites with web3 applications (dapps). AWS IoT is nothing more than one company's datacenters. Robonomics is a network that uses Polkadot, Ethereum, IPFS in its technology stack. All of the listed networks are globally accessible, not controlled by any country and available in every country.

RWS will provide the ability to transfer your device under control of the Polkadot network. This means that the security of startup, shutdown and telemetry transmission will be controlled by shared security on 1,000 nodes scattered across the planet. Figuratively speaking, your device will be controlled by a decentralized cloud.
